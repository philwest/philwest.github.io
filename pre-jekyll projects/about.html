About Phil West

Phil West is a writer, content maker, communications professional, and fancy eater, based in Austin, Texas.

He runs Orange Cone Agency, a full-service PR and digital strategies firm, which he's been doing since 2006. He also writes for two SB Nation blogs, The Short Fuse and Stars and Stripes FC. He's also working on a book about American soccer fandom and the 2014 World Cup called We Want the World.

He is a proud father of two amazing sons -- Noah, age 12, a Doctor Who fan and aspiring engineer, and Lucas, age 7, who is currently raving about Frozen and reading up a storm.
