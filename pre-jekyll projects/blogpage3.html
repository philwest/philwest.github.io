American Vs. American

For the last few months, I’ve been writing for Stars & Stripes FC, the SB Nation site covering U.S. soccer. Here’s my latest post, covering the Fulham vs. Sunderland match this Saturday — which doesn’t initially sound all that tempting for folks invested in English soccer let alone American soccer. But it features arguably the two best Americans in front of goal — Clint Dempsey, on loan to Fulham to help them get out of a rut for the two months he’ll be there, and Jozy Altidore, who might very well be in a goal-scoring rut for bottom-dwelling Sunderland right now, even though he’s looked brilliant for the USA against CONCACAF and European competition over the past ten months.

Want to see what Americans can do against “world-class players?” (That term gets thrown around quite a bit, as you might imagine.) Check it out on Premier League Extra Time at 10 am ET this Saturday.
